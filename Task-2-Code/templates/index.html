Who we are

Greenfield Local Hub is one of the largest farmers' cooperatives in the North West.

We aim to deliver you fresh, delicious seasonal produce whilst using methods that take care of our environment. This model of food production also supports the connection between local people and the food they eat, creating a food community and bringing everyone closer.

By becoming a member, you will be supporting the development of our co-operative, you will be able to join in!
